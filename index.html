📖 How to Play
Suika × Web3
Drop coins, merge matching pairs, climb the real Solana market cap ladder. Can you create the legendary ALPIE?
1
Aim & Drop
Drag left/right to aim — the dashed guide line shows the drop path. Release your finger (or mouse) to drop the coin. You can see your next coin in the preview box.
2
Merge Identical Coins
Two identical coins that touch instantly fuse into the next coin up the ladder. The merged coin earns points equal to its full value. Bigger merges = more points!
3
Chain Reactions = Huge Score
A merge can trigger another merge, which triggers another — a chain reaction! Plan your drops to set up cascading merges for explosive point bonuses.
4
Watch the Danger Line
The red line near the top is the danger zone. If any coin sits above it for 3 seconds straight — game over! Keep the pile low by merging often.
α
Create ALPIE — The Legend!
Merge two ORYNTH coins to create ALPIE — the #1 coin on the Orynth ecosystem by market cap. This triggers a legendary confetti celebration and unlocks your biggest points! 🎉

Pro Tips
🏗️Build a flat, stable base before stacking higher — uneven towers collapse fast and block merges.
⛓️Plan chain reactions: place a coin next to a matching one, which is already next to another matching one for a double merge.
🎯Use small coins (SYLLA, AUI) to fill tight gaps and set up merges on the sides of the pile.
📦The preview box top-right shows your NEXT coin — plan one step ahead to avoid awkward drops.
⚠️Red line = instant death zone. If coins linger above it for 3 seconds, the game ends. Clear the top fast!
🏆Unlock achievements by reaching new coins and hitting merge milestones — try to get them all!

What is MergeOrynth?
MergeOrynth is a physics-based merge puzzle game inspired by Suika Game — but with a Web3 twist. Drop real cryptocurrency coins from the Orynth ecosystem into the arena. When two identical coins collide, they fuse into the next coin up the Solana DEX market cap ladder.

Start with tiny SYLLABY coins and work your way up to the legendary ALPIE — the #1 project on Orynth by market cap. It's quick to learn, impossible to put down.
Real Coins, Real Market Caps
Every coin in this game is a real project built on orynth.dev — a launch platform for early-stage Web3 builders on Solana. The merge order follows actual DEX market cap rankings, sourced from live DexScreener data.
