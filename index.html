📖 How to Play
1
Aim & Drop
Slide left and right to aim. The dashed line shows where the coin will fall. Lift your finger (or release mouse) to drop.
2
Merge Identical Coins
When two identical coins touch, they instantly merge into the next coin up the market cap ladder — earning you points equal to that coin's value.
3
Climb the Ecosystem
Chain merges to create bigger and rarer coins. Each coin is a real project on Orynth — ranked by actual Solana DEX market cap!
α

Pro Tips
🏗️Build a strong base at the bottom before stacking higher — unstable towers collapse fast.
⛓️Drop coins near matching ones to trigger chain reactions for massive bonus points.
🎯Drop smaller coins into gaps to fill space and set up merges on the sides.
⚠️The red line = danger zone. If coins stay above it for 3 seconds, it's game over!

What is MergeOrynth?
Drop and merge real cryptocurrency coins from the Orynth ecosystem. Identical coins fuse into larger, rarer coins — climbing the real Solana DEX market cap ladder from SYLLABY all the way to legendary ALPIE.
Real Coins, Real Market Caps
Every coin in the game is a real project launched on orynth.dev — an ecosystem platform for early-stage Web3 builders on Solana. Market caps are sourced from live DexScreener data.
